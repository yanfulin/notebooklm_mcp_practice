# Flashcards: Rakuten Mobile — 5G MiFi Meeting Prep

**Q:** When did Rakuten Mobile launch the world's first fully virtualized, cloud-native mobile network?
**A:** In 2019, launching their full-scale commercial mobile carrier services based on Open RAN technology.

---

**Q:** What three companies did Rakuten partner with in July 2025 to deploy its 5G Standalone (SA) network?
**A:** Cisco (Mobile Packet Core), Nokia (cloud-native network functions), and F5 (security and traffic management).

---

**Q:** Which company provides Rakuten's nationwide 5G Open RAN radios including Massive MIMO and 700MHz band solutions?
**A:** Samsung was selected as the nationwide 5G Open RAN radio provider.

---

**Q:** What is the "Platinum Band" and why is it crucial for Rakuten Mobile's 5G network?
**A:** It is the 700 MHz low-band frequency that Rakuten commercialized in 2024 to significantly improve indoor and rural signal coverage.

---

**Q:** What is the theoretical maximum 5G download speed and maximum simultaneous connections for the Rakuten WiFi Pocket 5G?
**A:** 2.14 Gbps theoretical max download speed; connects up to 24 devices simultaneously.

---

**Q:** What type of SIM does the new Rakuten WiFi Pocket 5G use vs. the older 4G model?
**A:** The new 5G model uses eSIM (embedded SIM), whereas the 4G WiFi Pocket Platinum uses a physical nanoSIM.

---

**Q:** Who manufactures the Rakuten WiFi Pocket Platinum (4G LTE model)?
**A:** Fuyu Precision Component Co., LTD is the OEM manufacturer.

---

**Q:** What is the battery capacity and continuous usage time of the Rakuten WiFi Pocket Platinum?
**A:** 2,440 mAh battery providing approximately 10 hours of continuous LTE usage.

---

**Q:** According to CIAJ, what is the forecasted value of the Japanese router market by FY2030?
**A:** ¥134.4 billion by FY2030, driven by increased data traffic, corporate DX, and 5G/Wi-Fi 7 adoption.

---

**Q:** What is the projected growth rate and value for the Japanese mobile handset market by FY2030?
**A:** Expected to surge 15.6% to ¥3.6853 trillion in FY2030 as users adopt 5G SA-capable devices.

---

**Q:** What is the typical daily cost for a standard pocket Wi-Fi rental in Japan (2025)?
**A:** $4–$14 per day for a 7–14 day trip; drops to ~$3 per day for long-term rentals.

---

**Q:** Who serves as the Co-CEO and CTO of Rakuten Mobile, and is also President of Rakuten Symphony?
**A:** Sharad Sriwastawa.

---

**Q:** What was Rakuten's financial milestone for the Mobile segment in FY2025 Q4?
**A:** Record-high Rakuten Group revenue and achieved full-year profitability for the Mobile segment.

---

**Q:** What is the maximum monthly fee for unlimited data under the Rakuten SAIKYO Plan?
**A:** ¥2,980 per month for truly unlimited data.

---

**Q:** Which major Japanese carrier uses ZTE and Huawei for its "5G Speed Wi-Fi" lineup?
**A:** au (KDDI).

---

**Q:** How many subscribers does Rakuten Mobile have as of 2025?
**A:** Approximately 8–10 million subscribers (surpassed 8 million milestone in late 2024).

---

**Q:** How does Rakuten mobile integrate into the broader Rakuten ecosystem to attract users?
**A:** Subscribers earn 5x Rakuten Points on Rakuten Ichiba shopping, get up to 50GB free Rakuten Drive cloud storage, and free entertainment content bundles.

---

**Q:** What global export opportunity does Rakuten Symphony present for 5G MiFi hardware vendors?
**A:** Rakuten Symphony exports its Symworld integrated Open RAN solutions globally, creating opportunities to bundle compatible 5G MiFi devices for overseas operators.

---

**Q:** What aggressive promotional strategy does Rakuten Mobile use to sell 5G MiFi hardware?
**A:** Heavily subsidizes devices — frequently selling them for ¥1 when bundled with a new Rakuten SAIKYO Plan application.

---

**Q:** Why is the enterprise "Private 5G" market a major tailwind for Rakuten's 5G router business?
**A:** Enterprises increasingly demand uninterrupted, real-time data processing and hybrid Wi-Fi/5G edge connectivity for mission-critical automation and IoT.
