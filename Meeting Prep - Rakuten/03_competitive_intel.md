# Competitive Intelligence: Rakuten Mobile 5G MiFi

## Top 3 Things to Know About Rakuten's 5G MiFi Position

**1. Fully Virtualized 5G SA and Open RAN Ecosystem**
- Rakuten disrupted the industry with the world's first fully virtualized, cloud-native mobile network using Open RAN technology.
- Rolling out 5G Standalone (SA) network with Cisco (AI-driven Mobile Packet Core), Nokia (cloud-native network functions), and F5 (edge firewall and security).
- Samsung selected as nationwide 5G Open RAN radio provider — supplying low-band, mid-band, and Massive MIMO radios.
- **Your angle:** Emphasize our MiFi devices are natively optimized for multi-vendor, Open RAN 5G SA environments — seamless interoperability with the Cisco/Nokia/F5 core and Samsung radios is our key differentiator.

**2. Heavy Hardware Subsidization via the "¥1 Campaign" Strategy**
- Drives subscriber growth by aggressively subsidizing hardware — both 4G and 5G mobile routers offered for ¥1 when bundled with new plan.
- Actual retail price of new flagship Rakuten WiFi Pocket 5G is ~¥13,810; 4G LTE WiFi Pocket Platinum retails at ¥10,820.
- Currently relies on overseas OEMs like Fuyu Precision Component Co., LTD for low-cost device manufacturing.
- **Your angle:** Pitch our supply chain's efficiency and ability to deliver high-quality, high-volume 5G hardware at a rock-bottom BOM cost to protect Rakuten's margins while they subsidize devices to ¥1.

**3. Criticality of the 700 MHz "Platinum Band" and B2B Edge Capabilities**
- Rakuten launched commercial services using the 700 MHz "Platinum Band" — vital for improving indoor and rural coverage.
- New Rakuten WiFi Pocket 5G explicitly supports 700 MHz band alongside Sub-6 5G and 4G LTE, featuring eSIM instead of nanoSIM.
- Pushing into enterprise space — Private 5G and wireless edge computing demand high-capacity, secure routers (Pocket 5G supports 24 devices, 2.14 Gbps theoretical speeds).
- **Your angle:** Highlight our device's robust 700 MHz Platinum Band support, integrated eSIM capabilities, and enterprise-grade security for massive simultaneous B2B connections.
