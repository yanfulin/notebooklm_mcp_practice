# Rakuten Mobile – Company & 5G MiFi Device Profile
## Prepared for: Meeting Prep (5G MiFi Business)
## Date: March 2026

## 1. Company Overview

**Rakuten Mobile, Inc.** is a Japanese mobile network operator (MNO) and a subsidiary of Rakuten Group. It is globally renowned for launching the **world's first fully virtualized, cloud-native mobile network** in 2019 using Open RAN technology — a major disruption to the traditional telecom hardware market.

- **Headquarters:** Tokyo, Japan
- **Network Type:** Cloud-native, Open RAN, fully virtualized
- **Subscribers:** ~8–10 million (as of 2025)
- **Plans:** Rakuten SAIKYO Plan — tiered flat-rate plans (¥980/mo up to 3GB, ¥1,980/mo up to 20GB, ¥2,980/mo unlimited)
- **Co-CEOs:** Kazuhiro Suzuki and Sharad Sriwastawa (CTO)

---

## 2. 5G Network Strategy

Rakuten Mobile is actively building out its **5G Standalone (SA) network** across Japan using a cloud-native, AI-driven approach built on partner technology stacks.

### 5G SA Network Partners (Announced July 2025)
- **Cisco** – Mobile Packet Core Portfolio (AI-driven, high-speed 5G core)
- **Nokia** – Cloud-native network functions (CNFs): authentication, user data management, signaling, IMS voice
- **F5** – Security (edge firewall, DDoS) and traffic management CNFs for Gi/N6 LAN

### Radio Access Network
- **Samsung** – Nationwide 5G Open RAN radio provider
- **Rakuten Symphony** – Cloud-Native Platform and Storage for deploying 5G SA functions

### Frequencies
- Sub-6 GHz (5G)
- 700 MHz Band (Platinum Band – provides better indoor/rural coverage)
- 4G LTE (fallback via au/KDDI roaming)

## 4. Sales & Distribution Model

- **Online:** Rakuten Mobile website (main channel)
- **Retail Stores:** Rakuten Mobile Shops across Japan
- **Promotions:** Heavy use of 1-yen device campaigns bundled with Rakuten SAIKYO Plan subscriptions
- **Installments:** 24 or 48 months (0% APR for Rakuten Card holders)
- **Ecosystem Integration:** Deep integration with Rakuten Group (points, Rakuten Ichiba, Rakuten Drive cloud storage, entertainment content bundles)

## 5. Key Business Opportunities (5G MiFi Relevance)

- **Device Procurement / OEM Supply:** Rakuten Mobile sources devices from OEM manufacturers. The new Rakuten WiFi Pocket 5G represents a procurement relationship (manufacturer: TBD publicly, prior model by Fuyu Precision Component).
- **New 5G Device Pipeline:** As Rakuten aggressively pushes its 5G SA rollout in 2026, demand for 5G MiFi devices is expected to grow significantly.
- **B2B / Enterprise MiFi:** Rakuten Turbo 5G and upcoming 5G routers may be positioned for B2B use cases (branch offices, temporary connectivity).
- **Rakuten Symphony Exports:** Rakuten's telecom OS (Rakuten Symphony / Symworld) is being white-labeled and sold globally, presenting adjacent MiFi opportunities in international markets.
